At ClearQ, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mentorship platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better mentorship services to all our users. This includes:

  • •

    Personal identification (Name, email address, phone number).

  • •

    Professional details (Job title, company, skills, LinkedIn profile).

  • •

    Usage data (Search history, mentorship logs, communication metadata).

2. How We Use Your Information

ClearQ uses the data we collect for various purposes, primarily focused on facilitating successful mentorship connections:

  • •

    To match mentees with compatible mentors using our proprietary algorithm.

  • •

    To maintain and improve the technical stability of our platform.

  • •

    To notify you about changes to our services or upcoming sessions.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform, such as payment processors (Razorpay) and cloud hosting services (AWS).

4. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • •

    The right to access and export your data.

  • •

    The right to rectification of inaccurate information.

  • •

    The right to request deletion of your account and data.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • •

    Encryption of sensitive data

  • •

    Secure payment processing

  • •

    Regular security audits

  • •

    Access controls and authentication

  • •

    Secure data storage and backups

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • •

    Maintain user sessions

  • •

    Remember preferences

  • •

    Analyze platform usage

  • •

    Improve user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our platform.

7. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our data protection team.
