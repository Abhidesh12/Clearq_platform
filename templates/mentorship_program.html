Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our mentorship program

How is this different from regular mentorship?

Our program provides structured, goal-oriented mentorship with a defined curriculum, weekly progress tracking, and guaranteed outcomes. Unlike regular mentorship, you get a comprehensive career transformation plan with multiple support systems including mock interviews, job referrals, and certification.

Can I choose my mentor?

Yes! We match you with a mentor based on your career goals, industry, and personality. You can review mentor profiles and request a different mentor if needed. Our mentors are industry experts with proven track records in their respective fields.

What if I'm not satisfied with the program?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the program within the first 30 days, you can request a full refund with no questions asked.

Is financing available?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans. You can choose to pay in 3 monthly installments of ₹3,999 each instead of the one-time payment of ₹9,999. Contact our support team for more details on payment options.

How soon can I expect results?

Most students start seeing results within 4-8 weeks. However, this depends on your current level, dedication, and career goals. We provide continuous support for 3 months and additional alumni support to ensure you achieve your objectives.

What if I'm a complete beginner?

The program is designed for all levels - from complete beginners to experienced professionals. Our mentors provide personalized guidance based on your current skill level and career goals. We'll start from the basics and build up to advanced topics based on your progress.
